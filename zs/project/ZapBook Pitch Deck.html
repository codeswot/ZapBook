How it works

Three steps, one stream.

1

Read the book

A clean, distraction-free reader. Pages are real, structured text — your progress is tracked honestly as you go.

2

Pass the milestone

At each chapter end, a short milestone quiz checks you actually read it. Get it right, and the page is proven.

3

Get zapped

Sats land instantly — zapped over Nostr by a parent, grandparent, or your reading circle. Real bitcoin, in your wallet.

Why this stack

Real value. No platform in the middle.

Bitcoin is the reward

It holds value. Sats aren't points that expire — they're money the reader actually keeps.

Tiny amounts work. Lightning makes a 2,100-sat reward instant and nearly free to send.

Their wallet, their money. No store credit, no walled garden.

Nostr is the delivery

No accounts, no servers. Identities and zaps ride an open protocol — there's nothing for us to lock you into.

Circles are portable. Your reading group isn't trapped in ZapBook's database.

Zaps are native. Nostr already knows how to send sats between people. We build on it.
